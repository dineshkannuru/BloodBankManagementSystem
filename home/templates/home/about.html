About Us

BloodSource - Largest Community of donors

  • 2014-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started as a group of 10 people based in Mumbai who conducted small campaigns to educate people for blood donation and its importance

  • March 2016

    An Agency is Born

    As the group got bigger, we extended our program to different parts of city.As a result, our community started getting bigger and we got funds from various organisations like Bharat blood bank. Hence, BloodSource was born.

  • December 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    After running successfully in Mumbai, for a whole year, we started our branches in cities like Chennai ,Delhi, Agra, etc. We connected to some Hospitals to facilitate their blood requirements.

  • July 2018

    Phase Two Expansion

    We now plan to extend our services to all the main cities as well as small towns across the country to ensure that blood reaches the needy at the right time.
